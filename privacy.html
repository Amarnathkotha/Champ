# Privacy Policy for Scoring App **Last Updated:** January 14, 2026 ## Introduction This Privacy Policy explains how the Scoring App ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. ## Data Collection and Storage ### What Data We Collect The Scoring App collects and stores the following information locally on your device: - **Game Sessions**: Game names, creation dates, round counts, and archive status - **Player Information**: Player names and color assignments - **Scores**: Individual player scores for each round - **Player Pool**: Names of players you've added to your reusable player pool - **Settings**: Your preferences including theme (light/dark mode), haptic feedback, sound effects, and walkthrough completion status - **Win Conditions**: Your selected win condition (highest/lowest score) for each game ### How We Store Your Data **All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.** This means: - Your data never leaves your device - We do not have access to your data - Your data is not transmitted to any servers - Your data is not shared with any third parties - We do not collect, process, or analyze any user data ## Data Usage Your data is used exclusively to: - Display your game sessions and scores - Maintain your player pool for easy reuse - Remember your settings and preferences - Provide game statistics and analytics within the app ## Data Retention - Your data remains on your device indefinitely unless you manually delete it - You can clear all app data by clearing your browser's localStorage for this website - Archived games remain in storage until you manually delete them - Deleting the app or clearing browser data will permanently remove all your information ## Your Rights and Controls You have complete control over your data: ### Viewing Your Data - All your data is visible within the app interface - You can view all games, players, and scores at any time ### Editing Your Data - You can rename games and players - You can modify scores for any round - You can update your settings at any time ### Deleting Your Data - **Individual Games**: Archive or delete specific game sessions - **Players**: Remove players from your player pool - **All Data**: Clear your browser's localStorage to remove all app data ### Exporting Your Data - You can manually copy game names, player names, and scores from the app interface - Statistics can be shared via screenshot using the share feature ## Third-Party Services The Scoring App does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. There are: - No advertising networks - No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics) - No crash reporting services - No cloud storage services - No social media integrations ## Children's Privacy The Scoring App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and we have no access to user data, we cannot determine the age of our users. Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of the app. ## Data Security ### Local Storage Security - Data is stored in browser localStorage on your device - Access is limited to the app's domain - Security depends on your device's security and browser's implementation ### Recommendations for Users - Use device lock/password protection - Keep your browser updated - Use trusted devices when accessing the app - Be cautious when using shared or public devices ## Data Breaches Since we do not collect, store, or have access to any user data on our servers: - There is no central database to breach - Your data security depends entirely on your device security - We cannot notify you of breaches as we have no contact information - We recommend backing up important data manually ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be indicated by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through: - A notice within the app - An update to the "Last Updated" date - A summary of changes in the app release notes ## International Users The Scoring App operates as a client-side application: - No data crosses international borders as it never leaves your device - No servers are located in any jurisdiction - All data processing occurs locally on your device ## Legal Compliance ### GDPR Compliance (European Users) Since we do not collect or process personal data: - No data processing agreements are necessary - No data protection officer is required - Users have complete control over their local data - Right to erasure is exercised by clearing browser storage ### CCPA Compliance (California Users) - We do not sell personal information - We do not collect personal information as defined by CCPA - All data remains on users' devices ### Other Jurisdictions The app complies with privacy laws by design through: - No data collection - No data transmission - Complete user control - Local-only storage ## Technical Details ### Browser Storage - **Technology**: HTML5 localStorage API - **Scope**: Domain-specific (only this app can access its data) - **Capacity**: Typically 5-10MB depending on browser - **Persistence**: Data persists until manually cleared ### Data Format - Data is stored in JSON format - No encryption is applied (browser localStorage is not encrypted by default) - Users should not store sensitive information in player names or game names ## Cookies The Scoring App does not use cookies. All data storage is handled through localStorage. ## Open Source [If applicable] This app is open source.
